My Curious Mind

Picture this: a curious soul wondering, "What if Python and mathematics got married? What if their wedding was not only a spectacle but a project?" So, I embarked on a two-week adventure to unite these two loves of mine. And voilà—this project was born!

Every app here is a love letter to numbers and logic. Whether you're marveling at the mesmerizing Julian set or watching Dijkstra’s algorithm calculate the shortest path faster than you can say "Eureka," there’s something magical for everyone.

Exploring the Sub-Applications

Each sub-application is crafted to make you fall in love with mathematics. Click, explore, and immerse yourself. But hey, before you dive in, a word of advice:

Keep your internet speed fast and reliable! These apps love to work hard, but they need the support of a good connection to deliver their brilliance quickly.

Also, since these apps are built using Streamlit, they might occasionally take a nap (because even apps need rest, right?). If you see a button saying "Wake Up the App", give it a click to get things running smoothly!

Final Note

Whether you're a math enthusiast, a Python lover, or just someone curious to explore the intersection of the two, this project is for you. Have fun, learn, and let the numbers and logic sweep you off your feet!
