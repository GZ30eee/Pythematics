Frequently Asked Questions

Pythematics is a collection of Python-based mathematical tools designed to help users solve complex mathematical problems interactively. It includes optimization solvers, statistical analysis tools, encryption algorithms, and more.

To get started, simply visit the project's homepage and explore the available tools. You can interact with the applications directly in your browser. No installation is needed for most tools, but some may require a Python environment setup.
